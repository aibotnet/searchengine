Project Summary
 Tool which compare our product with product listed at different sites and extract reviews for products of our organization . I used python and it's libraries for developing this tool. After extracting reviews tool calculate review rating and generate the points(topics) which is best suited feature for a particular product depending upon the reviews we are getting.

Use of this Project
 The project is about extracting REVIEWS for as many products as possible falling under different categories. The project concentrates on the products shown at Nextag. For sameple i choosed 100 products.For All products, there are no reviews and hence no Wize rating because product rating is calculated based on the customer reviews.Product rating is the rating calculated by the company for any product and rating of a product has a huge impact on the customer decision regarding that product and influences the customer opinion in a good way.

So, content-matching techniques were required using which we can match our products on any e-commerce site and then can extract the reviews from that site. In this way, reviews for more and more products can be gathered and displayed on our website which not only helps the customers in deciding whether they want to make a purchase or not but also helps the company in calculating the rating of the product which influences the customer decision a lot. The idea is not to be dependent on the urls provided by the merchants for reviews but to come up with a technique which can extract reviews from anywhere on the internet.

Proposed Solution
 We have introduced a PRODUCT MATCHING algorithm which does web scraping. The algorithm acts as a crawler i.e. it crawls different websites chosen by us. Then according to the technique implemented, the algorithm finds if the product that we are looking for is available at the chosen site or not.

If available, the algorithm matches the products available at that website with our product. It then calculates the matching percentage of the found product with our product to be sure about the product found that it is in fact the product that we were looking for. If the product is matched, then reviews for that product are extracted.

Project Overview

A few domains corresponding to each category mentioned above are chosen as sample so that our product can be searched on these websites and if found, reviews will be extracted from them.

Products are chosen from Nextag website. Their information is taken from the company database, whatever is required to search them on the sample websites, and a search is made on those sites. According to the search results, first few links of the search result are considered and product matching algorithm is applied on the products found on first few links to check whether the product found in the search results is what we needed or not. If there are no results found for the product that was searched then we move on to the next product for that website.

If the product matches with our product and reviews are available for that product, then the corresponding page link is saved and reviews will be extracted from that product page.
